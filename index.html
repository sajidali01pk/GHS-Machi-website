Introduction

Since its establishment in 1990, Govt High School Machi has never looked back. It has progressed at an immense pace in a short space of time; the institution has maintained top distinctive status in S. S. C Exam held since 2021, conducted by BISE Mardan. The Govt High School Machi has extended itself by setting up Govt Science and Arts College in 2019 for spreading the light to the most oppressed class of our rural society. The School and College is located at Jafar Abad, Machi, on the Taja, Shewa Ada road, a short distance of 4 km from Rustam main Bazaar. Our High school section forms the backbone of our struggle to kindle and enlighten the hearts and minds of our children. At present the institution is exclusively meant for the science studies but keeping in view the utility of humanities, the administration is also considering the commencement of humanities classes in the near future. The institution is looking forward to serve the society, within the available resources and by the grace of Almighty Allah, our sincerity, devotion and the cooperation of our well- wishers, it will surpass many previous records of good schooling very soon.
